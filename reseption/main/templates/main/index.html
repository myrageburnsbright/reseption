    Question and Answer
    Our clients can pay as follows:
    • Cash/card at the office, in cities where the company has a representative office.
    • Cashless payment. Individuals and legal entities can pay by bank transfer. We will issue you an invoice after agreeing on all the details and signing the contract.
    You can place an order with us in any way convenient for you:

    • Call by phone: 8 800 444 36 72
    • Write to us in messenger, active links are indicated in the header of the site.
    • Order a call back and our manager will definitely call you back
    Delivery in Russia is carried out by transport companies. The price depends on the destination city, weight and dimensions of the cargo. The manager will tell you the approximate cost of delivery.
    This information can be provided to you by a TC employee or a manager of our company.
    Be sure to follow the instructions for receiving the cargo, which we send along with the contract. It describes in detail the scheme for receiving the cargo and what to do in case of dishonest delivery by the transport company.
    In case of detection of damage to the packaging and cargo upon delivery from the transport company, you have the right not to accept the cargo and refuse it. In other cases, if a manufacturing defect is detected, we will replace the parts at our own expense, if the conditions for acceptance and storage of the cargo before assembly were met.
    Yes, if you notify our company manager in advance, or if circumstances change unexpectedly, contact the TC yourself with a request to change the recipient. Please note that when specifying a legal entity as a recipient, a power of attorney or other documents confirming the right to receive are required.
    Since our products are not subject to mandatory certification, we cannot provide you with certificates for the products. But we do provide certificates for the materials from which our furniture is made.
    You can refuse an order for objective reasons, which you purchased from stock. However, in this case you will be obliged to pay the shipping costs (delivery cost) to the sender.
    If the furniture was made to order, then it will not be possible to refuse it without objective reasons.
