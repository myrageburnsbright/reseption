Crafting Luxury Bar Counters, Furniture, and Hardware

Furnibuild is a design-and-build studio specializing in statement bar counters, bespoke furniture, and premium hardware for hospitality and residential spaces across the United States.

Our Story

We started Furnibuild with a simple belief: great spaces begin with pieces that are built to last and designed to be remembered. From intimate home bars to high‑volume hospitality venues, we combine modern engineering with old‑world craftsmanship to deliver furniture that performs as beautifully as it looks.

Every project is guided by three principles—precision, honest materials, and service that respects your timeline and budget.

What We Build

  • Custom bar counters and back bars (wood, stone, metal, and mixed media)
  • Signature furniture: host stands, communal tables, banquettes, shelves, credenzas
  • Premium hardware: pulls, hinges, brackets, foot rails, and bespoke metalwork
  • Millwork and architectural details for restaurants, lounges, hotels, and residences

Materials & Craft

We select materials for their beauty, durability, and maintenance profile—then finish and engineer them for real‑world use.

  • Hardwoods: American Walnut, White Oak, Ash, Maple (FSC on request)
  • Metals: solid brass, stainless steel, blackened or powder‑coated steel
  • Stone & surfaces: marble, quartz, porcelain slabs, solid surface
  • Finishes: commercial‑grade catalyzed lacquer, hardwax oils, metal patinas, PVD
  • Joinery & engineering: moisture management, leveling systems, hidden access, cable/power integration

How We Work

  1. Discovery: share your plans, inspiration, dimensions, and target budget.
  2. Design & Engineering: drawings, material boards, and samples for approval.
  3. Build: handcrafted fabrication with progress updates and QC milestones.
  4. Deliver & Install: nationwide crated shipping or white‑glove delivery and on‑site support.

Typical lead times: 6–10 weeks for custom pieces, 7–14 days for in‑stock hardware. Rush options available.
